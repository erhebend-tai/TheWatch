TheWatch is a life-safety application that connects people in distress with verified nearby volunteers in seconds. When every second counts, TheWatch bridges the gap between an emergency and help arriving.

HOW IT WORKS

Set a personal emergency phrase. If you speak it, TheWatch instantly detects it through on-device speech recognition and dispatches 5-10 verified adult volunteers in your immediate vicinity to check on you. First responders are notified simultaneously (configurable). The entire process takes seconds, not minutes.

EMERGENCY PHRASE DETECTION
Set a custom phrase that triggers an SOS when spoken aloud. On-device speech-to-text means it works even with poor connectivity. Rapid-tap and movement-jerk detection provide additional trigger options when speaking is not possible.

VOLUNTEER RESPONDER NETWORK
Volunteers over 18 are geohash-indexed and cached for instant lookup. When an SOS triggers, nearby volunteers receive real-time notifications with directions. They can submit check-in reports with text, photos, and video.

ESCALATION CHAIN
TheWatch follows a 4-stage escalation protocol: initial dispatch, widened scope, emergency contacts, then first responders. Each stage has configurable timeouts. Nothing falls through the cracks.

REAL-TIME SITUATIONAL AWARENESS
Responders and your emergency contacts see live updates: GPS tracking, situation reports, evidence submissions, and responder status. All powered by real-time push notifications for instant updates.

WATCH CALLS
Request a community check-in without triggering a full emergency. Ideal for walking alone at night, arriving somewhere unfamiliar, or when something does not feel right.

DWELLING AND WORKPLACE PREPARATION
Submit details about your home or workplace: floor plans, exit routes, hazardous materials, mobility requirements. This information is securely stored and shared with responders only during an active emergency.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Location data is shared only during active emergencies. Authentication with 2FA protects your account. You control exactly what information is shared and with whom.

VOLUNTEER PROGRAM
Become a designated responder. Indicate your availability, whether you have a vehicle, and your proximity preferences. TheWatch matches you with nearby requests. You are never obligated to respond, but when you do, you could save a life.

TheWatch is designed with reference to ISO 27001/27002 information security standards. Our escalation protocols are modeled on industry-standard emergency dispatch procedures.

This app is free to use. Personal safety should be accessible to everyone.
